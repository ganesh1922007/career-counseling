Career Counseling

Join our comprehensive career counseling program for personalized guidance on your journey of self-discovery and career exploration. Through one-on-one sessions, discover your strengths, interests, and goals, empowering you to make informed decisions for your future success.

The Career Counseling Committee of Indian Language School plays a vital role in guiding students towards their future paths. Here are some of our agenda for the academic year 2024-25:

1) Career Guidance Sessions: We organise regular sessions where professionals from various fields speak about their careers, providing insights into different industries and job roles.

2) Personality and Aptitude Assessments: We will conduct assessments to help students understand their strengths, weaknesses, interests, and aptitudes, which can guide them in selecting suitable career paths.

3) Individual Counseling: We offer one-on-one counseling sessions for students to discuss their career aspirations, concerns, and doubts, providing personalized guidance and support.

4) Information Dissemination: Through our website, we will provide information about different career options, educational paths, scholarships, and employment trends through workshops, seminars, and informational materials.

5) College and Career Fairs: We will organise events where students can interact with representatives from colleges, universities, and various industries to explore their options and make informed decisions.

6) Resume Building and Preparation for University: We will assist students in crafting resumes, preparing for interviews, and developing essential documents that will enhance their chances of getting admission into their dream university.

7) Internship Placement Assistance: We will facilitate opportunities for students to gain practical experience through internships, and help them connect with potential employers.

8) Monitoring and Evaluation: Continuously assessing the counselling program's effectiveness through feedback from students and teachers, and making adjustments as needed to better serve the students' needs.

By providing comprehensive guidance and support, the Career Counseling Committee at ILS empowers students to make informed decisions about their academic and professional futures, ensuring they are well-prepared for success beyond school.
